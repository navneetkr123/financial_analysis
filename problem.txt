
Financial Forecasting and Risk Management
Your company is a leading financial institution that provides various financial products to its customers. To make informed decisions and optimize financial performance, it's crucial to accurately forecast future financial metrics and manage financial risks.
Objective

1. Develop a financial forecasting model that can predict future revenue, expenses, and profit.
2. Develop a risk management model that can identify and quantify financial risks.

Ideal dataset should contain the following data
The dataset includes features such as revenue, expenses, profit, market volatility, interest rates, and more.
